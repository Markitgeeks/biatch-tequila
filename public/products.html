The Rosa Blanco Story

Born From
Volcanic Soil

Rosa Blanco was the first expression we ever created — and it remains the heart of BIATCH. Named for the delicate rose-gold hue that catches the light when poured, this is a blanco that defies expectations.

Each bottle begins its journey in the highlands of Jalisco, where volcanic soil and altitude conspire to produce the sweetest, most complex agave in México. After 7–10 years of patient growth, the piñas are harvested by hand and slow-roasted for 72 hours before double distillation in traditional copper pot stills.
